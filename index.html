How It Works

Three Steps to a Cleaner City

1
Sensors Detect

IoT sensors embedded in every bin continuously measure fill levels, battery status, and environmental conditions, sending data every 15 minutes.

2
AI Optimises

Our AI engine processes sensor data, weather, traffic, and historical patterns to build the most efficient daily collection routes automatically.

3
Teams Execute

Drivers receive optimised routes on their devices. Operations managers track progress in real time. Reports are auto-generated after each collection cycle.
