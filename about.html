About SmartWaste

Our mission, technology stack, the team behind the system, and the story of building a greener city.

Our Mission

Building Cleaner Cities Through Technology

SmartWaste was conceived to tackle one of the most overlooked urban challenges — inefficient waste collection. By leveraging IoT sensors, AI algorithms, and real-time data, we help municipalities cut costs, reduce emissions, and improve quality of life for citizens.

Our platform is fully open-source, runs entirely in the browser without any backend infrastructure, and is designed for cities of every size — from small towns to metropolitan areas.

Our Environmental Commitment

Every feature we build is guided by one question: Does this make our planet greener? From optimising fuel routes to reducing overflow waste, our platform has prevented an estimated 12,000 tonnes of unnecessary CO₂ emissions across all deployments.
